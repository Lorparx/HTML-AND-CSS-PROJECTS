Deep Dish Skillet Pizza

Prep Time:

30-40 minutes

Level:

Medium

Deep dish pizza has got a lot going for it: a thick crust that's tender and chewy on the inside, but nicely crisped on the outside; a little extra room for piling on the toppings; and, deep dish is less needy than its thin crust style counterpart, which requires some care when rolling and stretching. Here, you just push the dough into the skillet and you're cooking. Save the leftovers for lunch!

Deep Dish Skillet Pizza

Cook time

30-40 minutes

Servings

6

Level

Medium

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pizza dough
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni

Instructions

Preheat the oven to 450°F (232°C). Grease a 12-inch cast iron skillet with olive oil. Roll out the pizza dough and press it into the skillet, making sure to cover the sides. Spread marinara sauce over the dough, then sprinkle with mozzarella cheese and top with pepperoni. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly. Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
